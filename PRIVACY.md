# Privacy Policy – CalmSpace

CalmSpace respects your privacy.

## Data Collection
CalmSpace does not collect, store, or share any personal data.
All app usage, journaling entries, and preferences remain entirely on the user’s device.

## Permissions
The app may request access to:
- Media library (only if the user chooses to select an image for their profile avatar).

These permissions are optional and are used only locally on the device.

## Third Parties
CalmSpace does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking services.

## Data Security
Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, no user data is stored on external servers.
