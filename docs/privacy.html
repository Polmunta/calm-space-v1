Privacy Policy – CalmSpace

Last updated: 13 Feb 2026

Overview

CalmSpace respects your privacy. The app is designed to work without accounts and without collecting personal data.

Data Collection

CalmSpace does not collect, store, transmit, or share personal data. Journal entries and preferences remain on the user’s device.

Permissions

The app may request access to the device’s media library only if the user chooses to select an image for their profile avatar. This permission is optional and is used locally on the device.

Third Parties

CalmSpace does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking services.

Data Security

Because no personal data is collected or transmitted, no user data is stored on external servers.
